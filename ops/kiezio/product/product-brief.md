# Product Brief

## Product

Kiezio is a safer hyperlocal community app for people who want to know what is happening nearby, ask local questions, share timely tips, and discuss neighborhood or campus life without turning every post into a permanent public identity artifact.

The core promise is: local relevance without location exposure, and pseudonymous speech with accountability.

## Target Users

- Students and young adults in dense local communities.
- Residents in urban neighborhoods who want practical local information.
- Newcomers looking for local recommendations and context.
- Event-goers, commuters, and campus communities with time-sensitive questions.

Start with one launch city or campus cluster. Do not launch nationally before moderation staffing and local-risk operations are proven.

## Key Differentiators

- Pseudonymous accounts with private accountability instead of "anything goes" anonymity.
- Coarse local feeds with k-anonymity, no exact distance display.
- Transparent moderation decisions with rule IDs and appeals.
- Community voting for relevance, not mob enforcement.
- Guarded user-to-user video chat with explicit request/accept, no open matching, and visible safety controls.
- Anti-doxxing policy that accounts for local context.
- Privacy and App Store compliance designed into the MVP.

## MVP Scope

Must have:

- Account creation with age gate, terms acceptance, and abuse policy acceptance.
- Pseudonymous local posting.
- Local feed by coarse area bucket.
- Comments and reactions.
- Report, block, mute, and hide-thread controls.
- Moderation queue and audit log.
- Appeal flow for removals, timeouts, and bans.
- Location permission onboarding with approximate-location fallback.
- Account deletion and data export request path.
- Safety copy for posting about identifiable people.
- User-to-user video call request from a local context, with consent gate, camera/microphone permissions, visible end/report/block controls, and no recording.

Should have:

- Local topic channels with creation limits.
- Verified local information posts for events, closures, transit, and safety notices.
- Private saved posts.
- Rate limits and cooldowns for new accounts.
- Trust-and-safety dashboards.

Out of MVP:

- Open anonymous direct messages.
- Dating, random matching, or "nearby people" discovery.
- Public karma score.
- Exact map of posts or users.
- Paid boosts, ads, or engagement-maximizing ranking.
- Cross-city "tourist mode" until abuse controls are mature.

## Success Metrics

Community quality:

- Median first moderation response time.
- Percent of reports resolved within SLA.
- Appeal overturn rate.
- Repeat offender rate.
- Doxxing and harassment incident rate per 1,000 active users.

Usefulness:

- Percent of feed posts with meaningful replies.
- Local question answer rate.
- Weekly active posters vs. passive readers.
- User survey: "I learned something useful nearby."

Privacy:

- Percent of sessions using approximate location.
- Count of exact-location storage events, target zero outside explicit safety/legal holds.
- K-anonymity threshold failures blocked.

Growth:

- 7-day and 30-day retention in launch community.
- Invite conversion by local cluster.
- Report rate trend as community grows.

## Non-Negotiables

- Do not claim full anonymity.
- Do not expose exact poster location or exact distance.
- Do not ship UGC without user-facing safety controls.
- Do not optimize ranking for outrage, dogpiles, or humiliation.
- Do not clone Jodel's brand, copy, UI language, or moderation assumptions.
