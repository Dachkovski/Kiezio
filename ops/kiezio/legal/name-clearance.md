# Name Clearance Notes

Research date: 2026-05-24.

This is a preliminary open-web and app-store screening, not legal advice and not trademark clearance. Before public launch, hire counsel or run a formal clearance search across DPMA, EUIPO, WIPO, USPTO, national registers, app stores, domains, and social handles.

## Recommended Working Name: Kiezio

Use `Kiezio` as the project and product working name.

Why:

- It is coined and visually distinct from Jodel, Yik Yak, Whisper, Nextdoor, Nebenan.de, and other obvious local-social references.
- It signals local neighborhood context through "Kiez" without directly describing the full service.
- Initial app-store and open-web checks did not show an obvious same-category app conflict for the exact name.
- It is short enough for app icons, bundle naming, and spoken references.

Risks:

- "Kiez" is a German common word and may narrow the brand to German-speaking markets.
- The exact term has sparse web usage, which is good for distinctiveness but still requires trademark search.
- Do not use a logo, type treatment, color palette, mascot, copy style, or UX language that resembles Jodel.

Initial technical placeholders:

- Product name: `Kiezio`
- Repository directory: `ops/kiezio`
- Future bundle identifier placeholder: `com.example.kiezio`
- Future app group placeholder: `group.com.example.kiezio`
- Future backend host placeholder: `api.kiezio.example`

## Rejected Or Deferred Names

### Naybo

Rejected. Search found an existing App Store app named Naybo in the social networking category and a related social meetup site. Same broad category creates unnecessary conflict risk.

Source: https://apps.apple.com/jp/app/naybo/id6755916005

### Nahbar

Rejected. Search found `nahbar.com` described as a social meetup app and other public uses. Too close to the intended space.

Source: https://www.ipaddress.com/website/nahbar.com/

### Orbiq

Rejected. Search found existing Orbiq software/tech businesses and a trademark record for ORBIQ covering data communication and software-related services.

Source: https://www.trademarkelite.com/europe/trademark/trademark-detail/014773477/ORBIQ

### Ankra

Rejected. Search found an App Store app named Ankra, a Kubernetes/software platform named Ankra, and trademark records. Too much collision noise.

Sources:

- https://apps.apple.com/us/app/ankra-ai-flashcards-quiz/id6757954500
- https://docs.ankra.io/

### Kiezfunk

Rejected. Search found existing podcast/music usage. Also too close to radio/broadcast semantics and not distinctive enough.

### Kiezklar

Deferred. It is a clear German positioning phrase, but search found existing phrase usage in commerce/editorial contexts. It is also more descriptive and less brandable than Kiezio.

### Nahsignal

Deferred. It is a strong metaphor for proximity, but search results show generic technical use. It may be weak for trademark protection because it describes a near signal.

### Ortivo / Ortly / Lokamo

Deferred or rejected due to existing business, app, or trademark noise found in quick search. They are not clearly better than Kiezio.

## Naming Guardrails

- Avoid terms related to yodeling, singing calls, mountains, yak, whispering, secrets, confessions, gossip, or "anonymous chat" as the main brand claim.
- Avoid names that imply dating, private matching, adult encounters, or untraceable anonymous behavior.
- Avoid descriptive local-only names that cannot be protected.
- Prefer coined names with a local hint and neutral safety posture.

## Formal Clearance Checklist

- Exact word mark search in DPMA, EUIPO, WIPO Global Brand Database, USPTO, UKIPO, Swissreg, and relevant launch markets.
- Similarity search for phonetic and visual variants: `Kiezio`, `Kiez.io`, `Kiezia`, `Kiezo`, `Kiezjo`, `Kizio`, `Kiez App`.
- App Store and Google Play exact/similar search in Social Networking, Lifestyle, Local, Events, Dating, and Communication categories.
- Domain check for `.com`, `.de`, `.app`, `.social`, and defensive typo domains.
- Social handle search on Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube, GitHub, Discord, Reddit, and LinkedIn.
- Legal review of Jodel, Yik Yak, Nextdoor, Nebenan.de, and other category trademarks to avoid similarity in name, icon, color, and category copy.
